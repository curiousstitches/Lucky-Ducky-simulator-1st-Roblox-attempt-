# 📣 SOCIAL MEDIA PLAYBOOK — Jeepers-Get-DUCKED

You have TikTok, Facebook, Discord, Roblox. Here's how each one earns you players.
On Roblox, **TikTok is the #1 growth engine** — short clips drive the most installs. Lean there hardest.

## The landing page (your link hub)
`landing/index.html` — drop it on Cloudflare Pages (free). Put ONE link to it in every bio.
Edit these placeholders before publishing:
- `ROBLOX_LINK_HERE` → your game URL (after you publish in Studio)
- `TIKTOK_LINK_HERE`, `DISCORD_LINK_HERE`, `FACEBOOK_LINK_HERE` → your profiles/invite

## 🎵 TikTok (priority #1)
Format: 9–15s vertical clips, fast cuts, trending sound, big text hook in first 1s.
Post 1–3x/day. Ideas that perform for Roblox sims:
- "Opening 10 eggs at once 🤯" (the x10 reveal flash)
- "I forged 100 ducks into ONE…" (Forge result reveal)
- "This is the RAREST duck in the game 🌈" (Secret/MEGA reveal)
- "Hardcore mode almost broke me" (gate grind + payoff)
- "You can DUCK other people's Jeeps 🚙🦆" (the social hook — very shareable)
Hashtags: #roblox #robloxsimulator #robloxgame #fyp #duck + your game name.
Hook lines (first text on screen): "POV: you found a rainbow duck" / "Rating ducks until I get a Secret".

## 💬 Discord

## 👍 Facebook
- Join Roblox parent/kid gaming groups and Roblox-fan groups; share milestone posts (not spam).
- Post update screenshots + the landing link. Good for reaching parents who approve playtime.

## 🎮 Roblox (on-platform)
- Polish ICON + THUMBNAIL first (biggest lever — see BRANDING.md).
- Use the description from BRANDING.md.
- Reply to every comment early — engagement boosts the algorithm.
- Add codes to the description so searchers find you.

## Launch week plan
Day -3: tease clips ("something's hatching 🥚") + open Discord.

Faces + reveals + a clear "wait WHAT" moment in the first second. That's what gets shared.
